Our mission:
To inform and inspire girls to innovate in the technology industry.

To achieve our mission, we host Mini-Camps, One-Day Camps, and One Week Camps.
Each experience covers our 3 piillars: INFORM, INSPIRE, INNOVATE.

  • Inform
  • Inspire
  • Innovate

Inform

Exposure is everything! Here at INTech, we find it extremely important for INTech Scholars to learn about the foundation of Computer Science and Technology.

INTech Scholars will learn about various forms of technology that span beyond mobile apps and tablets. Scholars will cover themes such as programming languages and thinking logically, among other topics.

Inspire

Hearing about someone elseâ€™s journey is always helpful. We bring in industry women who have a background in technology to share with INTech Scholars about their experiences and their paths.

The road to success is not straight so we want INTech Scholars to know that there are many ways that they can use technology to get to where they want to go. Our awesome women speakers share the journey that led them to technology, discuss their favorite projects, and give advice to INTech Scholars to foster creativity and motivation.

Innovate

The most exciting part of the INTech Camp is the innovation. INTech scholars are grouped together in teams to learn how to build technology solutions. Through teamwork, they are able to understand the importance of paired programming.

Teams are also assigned a tech mentor to assist in troubleshooting any issues they may have. Upon completion, the INTech Scholars present their findings, results, projects, etc. to their parents and the community.

What We Offer

  • Summer Camps

    INTech Scholars participate in a 5-day experience where they learn how to build websites. They are placed in teams in which they choose a social issue to focus on for their final product. In addition, Scholars network with women in the industry and participate in company tours.

  • One-Day Camps

    INTech Scholars spend a day engaging in our 3 Pillars, spending the majority of their time producing a technological product that revolves around a social issue of their choice.

  • Mini-Camps

    A 1-hour experience where women in the industry teach participants about technology and facilitate a CS-Unplugged activity in the classroom.

  • High School Club

    Stay tuned as we plan to pilot a High School Club for girls where they dedicate time after school to learn industry level technology platforms.
